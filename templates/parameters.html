📊 Course Recommendation Parameters

These are the factors considered by the system to recommend the most suitable course for students.

Study Hours (Hrs)

Represents the number of study hours per day, ranging from 1 to 5. Higher values indicate more study time and commitment.

Regularity

Indicates how consistent the student is in attending classes and completing assignments.
1 = Regular, 2 = Irregular

Interaction

Measures student’s interaction level with teachers and peers.
1 = Poor, 5 = Excellent

Time Management

Represents how well the student manages time for studies and other activities.
1 = Poor, 5 = Excellent

Grasping Ability

Reflects how quickly and effectively the student understands new concepts.
1 = Poor, 5 = Excellent

Previous Semester Result

Performance in the last semester.
1 = Fail, 5 = Distinction

SSLC Result

Performance in 10th-grade exams (SSLC).
1 = Fail, 5 = Distinction

PUC Result

Performance in Pre-University Course (PUC).
1 = Fail, 5 = Distinction
